# Morning Ritual

Start your day with purpose and clarity. This morning ritual is designed to awaken your mind, body, and spirit before the demands of the day take hold.

## Overview

A grounded morning sets the tone for everything that follows. This 15–20 minute practice helps you:

- Wake up intentionally instead of reactively
- Cultivate a positive mindset
- Focus on what matters most

## The Practice

### 1. Stillness (2 minutes)

Before reaching for your phone, sit quietly. Take three slow, deep breaths.

### 2. Gratitude (3 minutes)

Write down **three things** you are grateful for. Be specific — not just "my family," but _why_ and _how_ they enrich your life.

### 3. Intention Setting (5 minutes)

Ask yourself:

- _What is the one thing I most want to accomplish today?_
- _How do I want to feel at the end of the day?_

Write your answers down.

### 4. Movement (5–10 minutes)

Choose any gentle movement:

- A short walk outside
- Light stretching or yoga
- A few minutes of free dance

## Morning Checklist

- [ ] Stillness — breathe before you scroll
- [ ] Gratitude — write three specific things
- [ ] Intention — name your one priority
- [ ] Movement — get your body awake

## Tips

> Consistency matters more than perfection. Missing one day is okay — missing two in a row starts a new habit.
