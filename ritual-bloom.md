# Ritual Bloom

Welcome to **Ritual Bloom** — a collection of mindful practices to help you grow, reflect, and flourish every day.

## What is Ritual Bloom?

Ritual Bloom is a personal practice framework built around small, intentional habits that compound over time. Just as a flower blooms through consistent care and the right conditions, _you_ bloom through consistent rituals.

## Core Pillars

- **Presence** — Begin each practice with a moment of stillness
- **Intention** — Set a clear purpose before each ritual
- **Reflection** — Close each ritual with a brief review
- **Gratitude** — Acknowledge growth, no matter how small

## Ritual Guides

| Guide | Purpose | Duration |
|-------|---------|----------|
| [Morning Ritual](morning-ritual.md) | Set the tone for the day | 15–20 min |
| [Evening Ritual](evening-ritual.md) | Reflect and wind down | 10–15 min |

## Getting Started

1. Choose one ritual to begin with — don't try to do everything at once
2. Commit to it for **7 days** before evaluating
3. Keep a journal to track how you feel
4. Gradually layer in additional practices

> "Small rituals, practiced consistently, create extraordinary lives."
